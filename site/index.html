Oh hi, it's me!

A lot of you visiting this page know me in real life, but for those of you who don't: hello, I'm Em! I'm converting a school bus into a tiny home whilst simultaneously learning to code through a program called SheCodes. I had been posting my bus progress on Instagram but got off all Meta platforms in 2025 because ya girl don’t play those oligarchy games. Anyway that being said, I really missed updating friends and family through photos, so I figured might as well test my coding skills and put together a website to keep people in the loop! So yeah- welcome to the madness! 🤪

A little about me, for those who I don't know as well: I have three dogs and one cat, and my favorite thing is to be outdoors with them. I'm queer and use she/her pronouns. I enjoy animals, plants, books, making art, learning new things, jamming out to [often nerdy] music, exploring nature, annnnnd napping. 😌 I plan to live on the road with my little animal family once the bus is complete. The idea is we'll live nomadically until I find a place I like enough to stick around in. Spoiler alert, I'm betting it's somewhere in the southwest United States!

You can get to know the dogs over on The Wright Crew page, and you can follow more detailed bus progress on The Birdie Journey page. The glimmers page has fluctuated between more of a blog and quick jots about things that make me happy, so check it out if you're curious about my life, I guess? The coding should speak for itself and hopefully this site will get more impressive with time! 👉👉



Have questions or comments? I'd love to hear from you! You can reach me at: thenshefliesaway@gmail.com. Let's chat bus stuff, favorite travel destinations, dogs- or anything else! I love making friends and would love for the bus to help me find my people.
