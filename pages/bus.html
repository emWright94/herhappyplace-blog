All About The Bird

Birdie, aka "The Bird", is my 2000 TC1000 Bluebird school bus. She hails from Alabama and is slowly being converted into "home" for me and my crew. I flew 1400 miles to a state I'd never been to to pick her up sight unseen (except through photos and videos). I'd had a local mechanic check her out for me and he liked the look of her so I decided to go for it. My gambol paid off- Birdie is a solidly built girl with a strong engine (Cummins 5.9) and excellent transmission (Alison). She had zero rust on her undercarraige as she'd been in the south for her entire career.
